About the project

The War Service Bureau of Rutgers College was established in August 1917 and continued its operations through the end of the war to June 1919. Its mission was "to keep Rutgers students in contact with the college as well as with each other during World War I." 1

Explore selected correspondence from the War Service Bureau Records that has been transcribed, edited, and encoded by Rutgers Future Scholars below.
